# Clastone
- Clastone is a social tool for being able to accurately rank and view your previous/current/future watches of everything you have ever seen in a class-based superlist.

## Motivation
- I think a class system is more accurate than giving a number score to a given entry, as when you have seen 500+ movies it can be hard to get a grasp on what a good score to give it is.
- So this way, you can group together movies which are similarly liked, and within each class have a rough ranking of which is better or worse than anything else. The most important ranking orders are always at the top and the bottom.

## Where to Start
- Personally, I'd start by going through and adding as many items you can to unranked classes, and then starting to add in your "baseline" movies and shows. That is, your top and bottom ranked ones, and what you feel is a good indicator of each of the middle classes. This is bound to change but you need to just start laying an even layer over all classes so you can truly understand what it means for a given entry to belong in its given class.
